Security Tips

Avoid Personal Info: Don't use names, birthdays, or phone numbers.
Use Passphrases: Long phrases like Forest-Green-Running-99! are harder to crack than random words.
Unique for every account: Never reuse passwords across multiple platforms.
Enable MFA: Even a strong password should be backed by Multi-Factor Authentication.
